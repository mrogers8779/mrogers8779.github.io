🗂 HR Systems · Process Improvement

Recruiting & Onboarding Process Redesign

Parker Gas Company's recruiting and onboarding relied heavily on paper forms and scattered, disconnected processes across departments. Led the redesign of both functions within ADP Workforce Now, reducing recruiting to significantly fewer, more organized channels and digitizing key onboarding paperwork. The updated system also automatically notified key managers of potential hires, replacing a manual process of sending candidate information individually — reducing manual processing and creating a more consistent experience for new hires.
